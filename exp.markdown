## Academic Experiences

### Graduate Student Researcher, Princess Margaret Cancer Centre
#### Sep 2023 - Present
- **Supervisors**: Dr. Gregory W. Schwartz, Scientist, Princess Margaret Cancer Centre Research; Dr.
Chris McIntosh, Scientist, Toronto General Hospital Research
- **Project**: Repeatable gene expression inferences from live-cell phase contrast microscopy images using
machine learning
    - Developing machine learning models using PyTorch to infer molecular information from cellular morphology
    - Deploying methods as PyPI packages and Docker images for use by the scientific community.
- **Related GitHub Repos**:
    - [**SPAGHETTI**](https://github.com/YinniKun/spaghetti): A method for phase-contrast microscopy images pre-processing so that you can use your favourite H&E model on them through this domain adaptation method built with style transfer networks.

### Medical Physics Researcher, Grand River Hospital
#### Aug 2022 - Apr 2023
- **Supervisor**: Dr. Ernest Osei, Director, Department of Medical Physics and Radiation Safety
- **Project**: Mathematical simulation of glioblastoma growth dynamics through an agent based model
    - Used CompCell3D and ordinary and partial differential equations to model the growth dynamics of glioblastoma and identifying novel targets for treatment.

### Undergraduate Thesis Student, University of Waterloo 
#### May 2022 - Dec 2022
- **Supervisor**: Dr. Andrew Doxey, Associate Professor, Department of Biology
- **Project**: Investigating novel hosts and reservoirs of *Yersinia pestis* through metagenomic analysis of Sequence Read Archive
    - Written and run batch processing scripts on [Digital Research Alliance of Canada](https://www.alliancecan.ca/en) in Python, R, and Bash to identify signals on Sequence Read Archive for *Y. pestis*. 

### Undergraduate Researcher, University of Waterloo
#### Jan 2022 - Apr 2022 ; Jan 2023 - Apr 2023
- **Supervisors**: Dr. Brian Ingalls and Dr. Chris Bauch, Professor, Department of Applied Mathematics
- **Projects**: Mathematical modelling of SARS-CoV-2 host-virus dynamics and effects of antiviral therapies using ordinary differential equations; Mathematical modelling of COVID-19 disease dynamics in the Region of Waterloo
    - Written and run mathematical simulations of the SARS-CoV-2 in-host growth dynamics and COVID-19 diseases dynamics with ordinary differential equations using Python and R
- **Related GitHub Repos**:
    - [**SARS-CoV-2 in-host dynamics**](https://github.com/YinniKun/covid-treatment-model): A simulation of the in-host viral dynamics of SARS-CoV-2 written in R
    - [**COVID-19 disease dynamics**](https://github.com/YinniKun/covid-trajectory): A simulation of the disease dynamics of COVID-19 written in Python


### Research Associate, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University
#### Jan 2021 - Aug 2021 
- **Supervisor**: Dr. Xin Zhao, Professor, Department of Biomedical Engineering
- **Project**: Investigating novel bio-mimicking materials and structures for bone regeneration and long-term prevention of hypertrophic scars
    - Conducted in vitro experiments to test the effect of a 3D-printed scaffold for bone regeneration
    - Conducted in vivo animal experiments to test the effect of bio-mimicking materials for the long-term prevention of hypertrophic scars
    - Written manuscripts to present findings in both review and research manuscripts
